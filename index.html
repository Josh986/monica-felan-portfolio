About Monica

A Passion for Movement & Wellness

Monica Felan is a certified Pilates instructor with over a decade of experience helping people discover the transformative power of mindful movement. With a background in anatomy, kinesiology, and holistic wellness, she brings a deeply personalized approach to every session.

Whether you are recovering from an injury, looking to build core strength, improve posture, or simply find more ease in your body, Monica's method is rooted in precision, breath, and presence.

My Philosophy

"Pilates is not just exercise.
It is an education in self."

01

Breath First

Every movement begins and ends with intentional breath — the foundation of the Pilates method and the gateway to a calm, present mind.

02

Precision Over Repetition

Ten perfect movements will always outperform a hundred careless ones. We slow down to build the strength and awareness that lasts.

03

Progress, Not Perfection

Bodies are not machines. Monica meets you exactly where you are and guides you steadily forward, celebrating every milestone along the way.

04

Whole-Person Wellness

True transformation happens when body, breath, and mind align. Monica's approach honors all three — inside the studio and beyond it.
